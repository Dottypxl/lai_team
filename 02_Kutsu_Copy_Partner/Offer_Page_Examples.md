## 7. Offer page quality check

Before sending offer page copy, check:

- Is the offer named clearly?
- Is the reader named clearly?
- Does the page show what changes?
- Are the deliverables concrete?
- Is the process clear?
- Are boundaries included?
- Is there proof or context?
- Is the CTA natural?
- Is the language Luopua-specific?
- Can any section be removed?

## 8. Final rule

An offer page should not try to impress.

It should help the right person understand whether this is the right next step.
