# Luopua Forbidden Language

## 1. Purpose

This file defines what Luopua AI helpers should avoid.

If Luopua starts sounding generic, check this file.

## 2. Main rule

Do not use language that makes the work louder but less true.

Avoid phrases that sound polished but say nothing concrete.

## 3. Rebrand-specific avoid rules

Avoid using Japanese terms as decorative proof of depth.

Avoid explaining the brand through borrowed philosophy before explaining Luopua.

Avoid mixing too many symbolic systems in public copy.

Avoid making nature feel like a costume.

Avoid making the hand-drawn mark feel like imported symbolism.

Do not say:

```text
Luopua is inspired by Japanese philosophy.
```

Say:

```text
Luopua means letting go.
In practice, that means removing what hides the point.
```

Do not promise:

- guaranteed sales
- viral content
- fast growth
- dream clients
- total transformation

- passive income

- clear results without clear source material
- calm or wellbeing outcomes that the product cannot prove

## 13. Replace with Luopua language

### Instead of:

Elevate your brand.

Use:

Make your work easier to understand.

### Instead of:

Content that converts.

Use:

Content that carries one clear idea.

### Instead of:

Dream clients.

Use:

The right people.

### Instead of:

Show up online.

Use:

Build visibility from your own ground.

### Instead of:

High-converting website.

Use:

A website that knows what it needs to carry.

### Instead of:

Unlock your potential.

Use:

Name what matters and remove what hides it.

### Instead of:

Inspired by wabi-sabi.

Use:

Keep the useful marks of real work.

### Instead of:

Zen-inspired simplicity.

Use:

Leave room for the thought.

### Instead of:

Mutually beneficial partnership.

Use:

A partnership where the shared value is clear.

## 14. Before using a marketing or philosophy word

Ask:

- What does this actually mean?
- Could I say it more plainly?
- Does this sound like Kirsi?
- Does this help the reader understand?
- Is this borrowed language?
- Is this philosophy acting as practice or decoration?
- Is there a more concrete word?

## 15. Bad sentence examples

Bad:

Let’s elevate your brand with powerful content that converts.

Better:

Let’s make your offer easier to understand.

Bad:

Create a magnetic online presence that attracts your dream clients.

Better:

Build visibility that helps the right people recognize your work.

Bad:

Inspired by wabi-sabi and ikigai, Luopua helps you transform your brand.

Better:

Luopua helps you let go of what hides the point.

Bad:

Unlock your full potential with AI-powered systems.

Better:

Give your AI helper clear source material, role boundaries, and a job it can actually do.

Bad:

This unique opportunity gives your brand valuable exposure.

Better:

Your business would be visible to people who already value local culture, live music, and summer life in Suovanlahti.

## 16. Final check for every helper

Before sending output, ask:

- Is this phrase banned or close to banned?
- Does this sound like online business noise?
- Is the claim honest?
- Is the pressure real or manufactured?
- Is philosophy being glued on top?
- Could Kirsi say this?
- Can this be said more clearly?

## 17. Final rule

If a sentence sounds impressive but empty, remove it.
