## 2. Case: Luopua by Kirsi rebrand

### What it is

Luopua by Kirsi is the master brand structure for Kirsi’s services, products, and future tools.

The rebrand centres the brand on:

```text
Luopua = to let go
```

The architecture is:

```text
One house, many rooms.
```

### Why it matters for Luopua

This rebrand proves:

- the brand can hold both services and products
- product names can stay inside one master brand
- the visual foundation can stay consistent
- Kirsi’s own thinking can lead
- outside philosophies can inform the work without leading the public brand

### Useful angles

- letting go as method
- one house, many rooms
- products as rooms inside a master brand
- the hand-drawn mark as Kirsi’s hand
- clarity through less
- nature as ground, not decoration

### Useful phrases

Luopua means letting go.

One house, many rooms.

The mark belongs to the master brand.

The public brand does not need borrowed depth.

## 10. Fact boundary

Do not invent:

- exact visitor numbers
- ticket sales
- revenue
- sponsor promises
- names
- dates
- public claims
- client results
- testimonials
- product launch status

Use only confirmed facts.

If needed, write:

Kirsi should confirm the exact figure before publishing.

## 11. Case quality check

Before using a case, check:

- Is this fact confirmed?
- Does it support the point?
- Does it respect the people involved?
- Is it too private?
- Does it make the work clearer?
- Is the case being used as proof, not decoration?

## 12. Final rule

Use cases to ground Luopua.

Not to inflate it.
