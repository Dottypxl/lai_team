# Aava – FAQ Library

## 1. Purpose

This file gives Aava reusable FAQ answers.

FAQ answers should reduce uncertainty.

They should not sound like policy text.

## 2. FAQ answer structure

Use this structure:

```text
Question

Short answer

Context

Next step
```

Keep answers short unless the topic needs more detail.

## 3. General Luopua FAQs

### What is Luopua?

Luopua helps small businesses, cultural projects, and community-rooted work become easier to understand.

The work starts by finding the red thread, removing noise, and naming what matters.

This can support websites, content, offers, and AI helpers.

### Who is Luopua for?

Luopua is for small businesses, rural entrepreneurs, cultural operators, and community projects that need clearer communication.

It fits people who want visibility without becoming louder than the work itself.

### What does “red thread” mean?

The red thread is the one clear idea that holds the work together.

It helps decide what to say, what to remove, and what the next tool or page should carry.

### What does “own ground” mean?

Own ground means the part of your visibility that does not depend only on platforms.

It can include your message, website, email list, brand voice, method, and the way people recognize your work.

## 4. Luopua Clarity Session FAQs

### What is a Luopua Clarity Session?

A focused session for small businesses that need a clearer base before building more content, websites, offers, or AI tools.

You bring the messy material.

We find the red thread and turn it into practical next steps.

### What should I send before the session?

Send what you have.

This can include:

- website text
- offer notes
- old posts
- screenshots
- voice notes
- half-written drafts
- questions
- examples you like or dislike

It does not need to be polished.

The rough version is useful.

### What do I receive after the session?

You receive a practical clarity base.

Depending on the scope, this may include:

- red thread sentence
- offer summary
- audience notes
- message angles
- website or content direction
- next-step recommendations

### Is this a full brand strategy?

No.

The Clarity Session is a focused first step.

It helps name the base before bigger work.

### Is this a website project?

No.

It can help prepare for a website, but it does not include website design or build unless agreed separately.

### Can this help with AI?

Yes.

Clear source material helps AI produce more useful and more on-voice output.

The session can create source text for future AI helpers or prompts.

## 5. Website Clarity Review FAQs

### What is a Website Clarity Review?

A review of your website through Luopua's clarity lens.

It looks at what the site is trying to carry, what the reader understands, where the offer feels unclear, and what to fix first.

### Is this a technical audit?

No.

This is mainly about message, reader path, offer clarity, and content structure.

Technical issues can be noted if visible, but they are not the main focus unless agreed.

### Do I need a finished website?

No.

You can send a live website, draft page, or planned page structure.

### Will you rewrite the full website?

Not in the review.

The review gives priorities and clarity notes.

Full copywriting can be scoped separately.

## 6. AI Helper Setup FAQs

### What is an AI helper?

An AI helper is a defined assistant role with clear instructions, source material, voice boundaries, and a workflow.

It is not just a prompt.

### Why does an AI helper need a role?

Without a role, AI becomes another place to make decisions.

A clear role helps the helper know what to do, what to protect, and where to stop.

### Can you build my whole AI team?

Yes, but it should start with one helper.

A full team makes sense only after the first role, workflow, and source material have been tested.

### Will AI replace my voice?

Not if the source material and boundaries are clear.

The goal is to protect your voice, not flatten it.

## 7. Process FAQs

### How do we start?

Start by sending a short note about what feels unclear.

If there is a clear fit, the next step may be a clarity session, review, or project call.

### Do I need to know what service I need?

No.

You can describe what feels messy.

Kirsi can help identify the right next step.

### Can I send messy material?

Yes.

Messy material is often the best starting point.

It shows where the red thread is hiding.

### How fast do you reply?

Reply time depends on current workload.

If a deadline matters, include it in your message.

### Do you work with businesses outside rural areas?

Yes.

Luopua is rooted in rural and community-aware thinking, but the method can help any small business that wants clearer communication without hype.

## 8. Boundary FAQs

### Can I ask for extra work during a project?

Yes, but extra work may need a separate price or later phase.

If the request changes the scope, Kirsi will name that clearly before continuing.

### What happens if I send material late?

Late material may affect the timeline.

Kirsi will tell you what can still be done and what may need to move.

### Can I get unlimited revisions?

No.

Revision rounds depend on the agreed scope.

Extra revisions can be priced separately if needed.

### Can you manage all my social media?

Not as the main Luopua offer.

Luopua focuses on clarity, content direction, source material, and systems.

Ongoing production can be discussed only with clear boundaries.

## 9. Payment FAQs

### When do I pay?

For smaller sessions, payment is usually made before booking or delivery.

For larger projects, payment can be split into phases.

### Can I get a discount?

Discounts are not the default.

Reduced pricing may be possible for pilot work, community projects, barter with real value, or reduced scope.

### Why is a clarity session not priced like one hour of talking?

Because the work includes preparation, thinking, the session, and written output.

The visible meeting is only part of the work.

## 10. FAQ source rule

When Aava creates FAQ text, include only confirmed facts.

If pricing, timeline, or scope is not confirmed, write:

This depends on the agreed scope.

Or:

Kirsi will confirm this before the work begins.

## 11. FAQ quality check

Before saving a FAQ answer, check:

- Is the answer direct?
- Is it short enough?
- Does it avoid policy language?
- Does it protect boundaries?
- Does it name the next step?
- Is anything unconfirmed?
- Does it sound like Luopua?

## 12. Final rule

FAQ text should make the next question lighter.
