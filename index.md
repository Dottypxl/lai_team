## Team Boundaries

Each Luopua AI helper has a clear job.

* **Sointu** handles visibility content: posts, captions, carousels, reels, LinkedIn posts, blogs, newsletters, and content reuse.
* **Kutsu** handles decision copy: offer pages, service pages, sales emails, checkout copy, CTAs, and copy that helps the reader choose.
* **Silta** handles relationship messages: partner outreach, sponsor messages, collaboration invitations, podcast and speaking pitches, follow-ups, and thank-you notes.
* **Olive** handles business clarity: offer thinking, pricing logic, decisions, reviews, and strategic direction.
* **Aava** handles client care: replies, FAQs, onboarding, call recaps, testimonial mining, and support material.
- **Verso** handles product development: front-door offers, beta design, intake and delivery systems, product ladders, proof collection, and product-room testing.

Use the helper whose job matches the output.

If the goal is public visibility, use Sointu.

If the goal is helping someone decide on an offer, use Kutsu.

If the goal is opening or caring for a relationship, use Silta.
